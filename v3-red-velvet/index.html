The Premise

A Game of Belief

Equal parts dinner party, debate, and grand performance. Each guest arrives entrusted with a single theory — assigned in advance — to defend before the assembled company.

You may believe it completely. You may find it gloriously absurd. It makes no difference. Tonight there is one prize, one stage, and one question: who can make the whole room believe?
