An Evening, in Four Chapters

You Are Cordially Suspected

A meticulously suspicious soirée, arranged with great care.

You will be assigned one theory, in advance, and asked to defend it before the assembled company — with conviction, charm, and the visual aids of your choosing. The most persuasive guest shall be awarded a genuinely valuable prize. Punctuality is admired; honesty is optional.

Chapter One

The Premise

A small assembly. One absurd theory apiece. A single, simple objective: persuade the room. You may believe your theory wholeheartedly, or find it entirely preposterous. The company cannot tell the difference — and that, dear guest, is rather the point.

Your assignment arrives ahead of time. Study it thoroughly. A prepared, well-mannered argument is expected.

II.

The Medium Is Yours
