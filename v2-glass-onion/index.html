IThe Premise

A Game of Belief

Somewhere between a dinner party and an interrogation, a small assembly will gather under one roof. Each guest arrives holding a secret: a theory, assigned in advance, that they alone must defend.

You may believe it completely. You may find it perfectly absurd. It makes no difference. Tonight there is only one question worth asking — and only one mind clever enough to make us believe the answer.
